FUN HOME: A FAMILY TRAGICOMIC

Title: Fun Home: A Family tragicomic

Author: Alison Bechdel

Publisher: Mariner Books

Genre: memoir

Number of Pages: 232

Rating: 5

Synopsis: In this graphic memoir, Alison bechdel charts her fraught relationship with her late father.

Distant and exacting, Bruce Bechdel was an English teacher and director of the town funeral home, which Alison and her family referred to as the fun home. It was not until college that Alison, who had recently come out as lesbian, discovered that her father was also gay. A few weeks after this revelation, he was dead, leaving a legacy of mystery for his daughter to solve.
